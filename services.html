COMMON QUESTIONS

Questions worth asking any agency

What is incrementality testing?

Incrementality testing measures whether advertising drives sales that would not have happened anyway. It compares outcomes with and without ad exposure — through geo holdouts, spend pauses, or controlled experiments — so a business can see what its media budget actually adds, rather than what platforms claim credit for.

What does the independent audit include?

A full review of paid search and programmatic accounts: measurement and attribution, and wasted spend across campaigns, keywords, and placements. It's delivered as a written brief with prioritised recommendations, and it stands on its own whether or not the engagement continues.

What budgets does Mind & Metric Media work with?

Typically businesses spending $5,000 to $100,000+ per month on paid media. Smaller budgets are considered where the measurement question is worth answering.

Are there lock-in contracts?

No. Engagements run as retainers or projects, and all reporting is in plain numbers — if the work isn't returning, the numbers will say so.
